The primary way to store settings for an app is to save them as key-value pairs in either Windows.Storage.ApplicationData.current.localSettings or Windows.Storage.ApplicationData.current.roamingSettings. Use localSettings for settings that are specific to a device, or use roamingSettings to transition settings between a user's PCs. For roaming, also refer to the HighPriority and the DataChanged Event scenarios.

This scenario writes, reads, and clears a setting in roamingSettings.
